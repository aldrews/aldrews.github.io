Introduction

One of the most commonly used technologies within the modern day sphere of technology is the browser. More specifically, the technology that encompasses an individual's browsing experience. When someone uses the internet, one of the most common applications is a browser such as Google. This is one of countless options in search engines and methods of “surfing the web.” In the short span of time that computers have been part of everyday lives, browsing has become a pivotal resource of our minute by minute existence and a nearly essential tool in how people operate every day.

Key Points

Browser:

A computer program with a graphical user interface for displaying and navigation between web pages.

Search Engine:

A program that searches for and identifies items in a database that correspond to keywords or characters specified by the user, used especially for finding particular sites on the internet.

Surfing:

The act of casually browsing, searching, or navigating through various websites and web pages on the internet.

AI:

Artifical Intelligence, or computer systems designed to simulate human cognitive functions.

Cybersecurity:

The practive of protecting systems, networks, programs, and dtat from digital attacks, unauthorized access, or damage.
